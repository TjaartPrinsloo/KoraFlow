# SOP: Access Governance & Review

## 1. Purpose
To periodically validate that all active accounts are legitimate and that permissions are aligned with current job responsibilities (Least Privilege).

## 2. Frequency
*   **Quarterly**: Full user access review.
*   **Ad-hoc**: After major organizational restructuring.

## 3. The Process

1.  **Generate List**: IT Admin generates a list of all `Enabled` users and their Roles from Frappe and GCP.
2.  **Review**: Department Heads review the list for their teams.
    *   *Check 1*: Is the user still employed?
    *   *Check 2*: Is the role still appropriate?
3.  **Correction**:
    *   Mark users for Revocation or Role Adjustment.
    *   IT Admin executes corrections immediately.
4.  **Sign-off**: System Owner signs off the final review report.

## 4. Outputs (Evidence)
*   **Access Review Report**: Spreadsheet snapshot of permissions at that date.
*   **Correction Tickets**: Links to any remediation tickets created.
